What is a Bondit and why should I use it?

Bondit is a class scheduling and socializing application. In Bondit, you can easily add your courses and check and communicate with classmates. More than 72% of college students still use outdated methods(school web, ppt, excel, notion etc) to create their class schedules. In Bondit, you can easily search and add your courses on your weekly calendar.

Who can use Bondit?

Only verified college students can use Bondit. Users must get a verification by using their college email, college certification, or referral code.

Is Bondit a secure app?

Yes, we are secure app. Our corporation is officially built in Delaware and got angel funds from well known investors/institutions.

Is it safe to share my location?

All users must verify themselves with their college email address. Therefore, there will be no strangers other than college students. Also you can turn on/off your location. All of your personal datas will be incoded. Also we will never overuse your data.
